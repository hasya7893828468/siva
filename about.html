Becoming Shiva, from Jiva

Param Pujya Dadashri explained that identifying oneself as the relative self is “jiva”, and identifying one with the real Self (the Soul, the Atma) through awareness is called “Shiva”. A seeker asked, “If Atma (the Self) was a Shiva, how did it become a jiva?” Param Pujya Dadashri replied, “Due to wrong belief, it became jiva - that which lives and dies.
